A Strong Research Culture

BBIS encourages curiosity, analytical thinking, and undergraduate participation in real-world research. Students engage in guided studies, faculty-led research groups, and opportunities to present at internal and external forums.

Faculty & Undergraduate Collaboration

Students work with faculty on applied research in areas such as Information Systems, AI Applications, Digital Transformation, and Data Analytics. They learn research design, methodology, and industry relevance through supervised work.

  • Research methodology courses with hands-on assignments
