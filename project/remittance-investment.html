Factors Influencing Productive Investment of Remittance in Nepal

Author: Subigya Adhikari

Published:2024

Abstract

This study investigates how remittance income is utilized by recipient households in Nepal, specifically focusing on productive investments versus consumption. It identifies barriers such as lack of financial literacy, limited investment opportunities, and market volatility that hinder productive use of funds. Recommendations are provided for policymakers to channel remittance towards sustainable economic development.
