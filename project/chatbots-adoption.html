Factors Motivating Nepalese Customers to Use Chatbots

Author: Abhishek Byanjankar

Published: November 2024

Abstract

This study identifies the key factors motivating Nepalese customers to adopt chatbot technology in various service sectors. Using the Analytic Hierarchy Process (AHP) approach, the research ranks factors such as 24/7 availability, quick response times, and ease of use. The findings provide valuable insights for businesses looking to implement or improve their automated customer service solutions.
