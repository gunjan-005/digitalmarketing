Impact of Misleading Internet Advertising on Consumer Behavior

Author: Isabella Neupane

Published: 2023

Abstract

This research investigates the prevalence of misleading internet advertisements and their profound impact on consumer behavior. It examines how exaggerated claims, hidden costs, and false endorsements affect consumer trust and purchasing intent. The study emphasizes the need for stricter regulations and increased consumer awareness to combat deceptive digital marketing practices.
