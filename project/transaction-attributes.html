Impact of Transaction Attributes on Online Transactions

Author: Trilochan Adhikari

Published: 2023

Abstract

This research examines the critical attributes that influence the success of online transactions for both retailers and customers. Key factors analyzed include security, convenience, transaction costs, and perceived trust. The study provides a comprehensive framework for understanding the determinants of e-commerce adoption and satisfaction.
