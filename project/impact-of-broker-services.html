Study on Impact of Broker Services on Investor Satisfaction in Nepal

Author: Pratik Sipkhan

Published: November 2024

Abstract

This research examines how brokerage services influence investor trust, satisfaction, and decision-making in Nepal’s capital market. It investigates the relationship between service quality dimensions—such as reliability, responsiveness, and assurance—and investor loyalty. The study employs a quantitative approach to analyze data collected from active investors, providing insights for brokerage firms to improve their service delivery.
