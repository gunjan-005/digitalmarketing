Pattern of Internet Usage: A Study of University Students

Author:Hima Karki

Published: 2023

Abstract

This study explores the internet usage patterns of university students, focusing on the frequency, duration, and purpose of their online activities. It categorizes usage into academic research, social networking, entertainment, and skill development. The findings shed light on the digital habits of the youth demographic and their implications for educational institutions and service providers.
