Role of Government Policies in Mobile Payment System Growth

Author: Ezal Sujakhu

Published: November 2024

Abstract

This paper analyzes how government regulations and policies have impacted the transaction frequency and volume within Nepal’s mobile payment ecosystem. It explores the effects of interoperability guidelines, transaction limits, and digital literacy campaigns initiated by the central bank. The results highlight the critical role of supportive policy frameworks in fostering a cashless economy.
