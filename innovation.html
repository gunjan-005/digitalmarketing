Innovation & Hackathon Activities

BBIS fosters creativity, problem-solving, collaboration, and real-world development through annual hackathons, bootcamps, challenges, and incubation programs.

Overview

Innovation lies at the heart of BBIS. The program hosts multiple events every year to encourage creativity, systems thinking, teamwork, and hands-on development. These activities strengthen student innovation KU and prepare learners for real-world tech challenges.

Annual Hackathon Events

1. KU SOM 24-Hour Tech Challenge

  • Theme: Smart Cities
  • Duration: 24 hours
  • Winner: Team Sync (Smart waste route optimizer)

2. Business Data Analytics Hackathon

  • Theme: Retail Analytics
  • Outcome: Dashboards, ML models, forecasting tools

3. Innovation Bootcamp Series

  • Sessions: Design Thinking, UX, Pitching
  • Outcome: 15+ student startup ideas incubated
