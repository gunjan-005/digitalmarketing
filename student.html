BBIS Student Research

Explore student research papers, case studies, and the project journey that shapes analytical, system-building, and research-driven graduates at KU SOM.

Study on Impact of Broker Services on Investor Satisfaction in Nepal
Examines how brokerage services influence investor trust, satisfaction, and decision-making in Nepal’s capital market.
Factors Motivating Nepalese Customers to Use Chatbots
Identifies key factors driving chatbot adoption using the Analytic Hierarchy Process (AHP) approach.
Role of Government Policies in Mobile Payment System Growth
Analyzes how regulations and policies impact transaction frequency and volume in Nepal’s mobile payment ecosystem.
Factors Influencing Productive Investment of Remittance in Nepal
Studies how remittance income is invested productively and the challenges affecting economic development.
Impact of Misleading Internet Advertising on Consumer Behavior
Investigates how deceptive online advertisements influence consumer perception, trust, and purchasing decisions.
Pattern of Internet Usage: A Study of University Students
Explores internet usage behavior, purposes, and digital habits among university-level students.
Impact of Transaction Attributes on Online Transactions
Examines how security, convenience, cost, and trust affect online transactions for retailers and customers.
